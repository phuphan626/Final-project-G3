ML Features

These particular features were used as they were assumed as the best indicators of visualizing the US economy and market - using daily and monthly values that showed relative indices and percentages that would best reflect the shifting prices in the US Bitcoin market.

US DOLLAR INDEX

Used to measure the value of the dollar against a basket of six world currencies - Euro, Swiss Franc, Japanese Yen, Canadian dollar, British pound, and Swedish Krona. The value of the index is fair indication of the dollarâ€™s value in global markets. It is a weekly measure used to determine if real US dollar value across te global market effects the shift of value of the cryptocurrency market..

NASDAQ INDEX

is a large market-cap-weighted index of more than 2,500 stocks constantly throughout the trading day with the final value reported at 4:16 p.m. daily once prices have fully settled after the 4:00 p.m. ET market close. NASDAQ's composition is nearly 50% technology, with consumer services, health care and financials the next most prominent industries.This daily value is measure to test against the movement and price of Bitcoin

S&P500 INDEX

a market-capitalization-weighted index of the 500 largest U.S. publicly traded companies. The S&P is a float-weighted index, meaning company market capitalizations are adjusted by the number of shares available for public trading. It is widely regarded as the best gauge of large-cap U.S. equities. As a result, there are many funds designed to track the performance of the S&P. This is another daily value we extract to determine its influence in shifting the price of Bitcoin.

DOW JONES INDUSTRIAL INDEX

is a widely-watched benchmark index in the U.S. for blue-chip stocks. It is a price-weighted index that tracks 30 large, publicly-owned companies trading on the New York Stock Exchange and the NASDAQ. The index was created by Charles Dow in 1896 to serve as a proxy for the broader U.S. economy. This is the third Stock Market index value used for its daily values to try to capture its influence on Bitcoin price.

US UNEMPLOYMENT RATE

is the proportion of the labor force that is not currently employed but could be. It is a Monthly percentage that can show the state of the economy and effect on the other variables to see if that effects the increase or decrease in Bitcoin prices.

PRICE OF GOLD

investment demand, especially from large ETFs, is another factor underlying the price of gold.Gold sometimes moves opposite to the U.S. dollar because the metal is dollar-denominated, making it a hedge to against inflation. as of Septmeber 2020, Gold Price hit a record high of 70% correlation to Bitcoin, during the same year in which the US Dollar was falling against other major currencies.
